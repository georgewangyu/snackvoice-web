Privacy Policy

SnackVoice is built around local transcription on your Mac. This page covers the website and purchase flow.

What we collect

When you purchase SnackVoice, we may collect your email address, payment status, license information, and basic order metadata needed to deliver the app and support the license.

What we do not collect

SnackVoice is positioned as a local-first dictation app. Your spoken audio and local transcripts are not intended to be uploaded by the website purchase flow.

Payments and vendors

Payments are processed by Stripe. License delivery may use third-party services such as Keygen and an email provider. Their handling of your data is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

Support

If you need help with delivery, licensing, or data questions, contact support@snackvoice.app.
